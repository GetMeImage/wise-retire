⚠ RetireWise provides educational projections — not financial advice. FY2025-26 New Regime slabs: ₹0–4L 0%, ₹4–8L 5%, ₹8–12L 10%, ₹12–16L 15%, ₹16–20L 20%, ₹20–24L 25%, above ₹24L 30%. Sec 87A rebate: zero tax if combined taxable income ≤ ₹12L. 4% health & education cess applied. Surcharge 10% for income >₹50L, 15% for >₹1Cr. NPS: non-govt subscribers 80% lump sum tax-free (Sec 10(12A)), 20% mandatory annuity taxable. Govt employees: 60% lump sum. LTCG on MF equity: 12.5% on gains above ₹1.25L/yr (Budget 2024). SCSS interest fully taxable. EPF tax-free after 5+ yrs continuous service. Gratuity: tax-free up to ₹20L for private sector employees (Sec 10(10)); fully tax-free for govt/PSU. Consult a SEBI-registered adviser (RIA) before decisions.
